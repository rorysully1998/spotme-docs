1. Overview

SpotMe is a comprehensive social fitness app that combines nutrition tracking, workout logging, body composition analysis, and social features into one platform. Users can track their fitness journey, compete with friends, and earn achievements along the way.

Key Features at a Glance

  • Complete macro & calorie tracking
  • Barcode scanning & nutrition label OCR
  • Gym workout logging with splits
  • GPS cardio tracking
  • AI-powered body composition analysis
  • Social feed & training partners
  • 1v1 fitness challenges
  • 200+ achievements to earn
  • Global leaderboard & rankings
  • Apple Health & Health Connect sync

2. Account & Profile

Registration & Login

  • Create an account with email and password
  • Secure authentication via Firebase
  • Select your fitness goal on signup: Cutting, Bulking, Maintenance, or Recomp

Profile Customization

  • Set username and bio
  • Upload profile picture
  • Choose which stats are visible to others (age, weight, height, body fat, muscle mass, fitness class)
  • Public or private profile options

Profile Display

  • View your posts in grid format
  • See your training partners and followers count
  • Display current body stats and fitness class
  • Showcase earned awards
  • View rank badge and tier status

3. Nutrition & Calorie Tracking

Daily Food Logging

  • Log meals by category: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner, Snacks
  • Navigate between different dates
  • View daily totals and progress toward goals

Smart Food Entry Methods

Method Description
Food Database Search Search our extensive built-in food database
Barcode Scanner Scan product barcodes to instantly log nutrition info (powered by USDA FoodData Central)
Nutrition Label OCR Take a photo of any nutrition label and our AI will automatically extract the values
Manual Entry Enter custom foods with full macro information

Comprehensive Macro Tracking

Track all essential nutrients:

  • Macros: Calories, Protein, Carbohydrates, Fat, Saturated Fat, Trans Fat, Fiber, Sugar, Added Sugar
  • Micronutrients: Vitamin D, Calcium, Iron, Potassium, Cholesterol, Sodium

Goals & Progress

  • Set daily calorie targets
  • Set weekly macro goals with min-max ranges
  • Visual progress bars with color coding (green = on track, red = over/under)
  • Automatic calorie offset from cardio workouts
  • Macro calendar to view historical data

4. Workout Tracking

Gym / Weight Training

Workout Splits

  • Create custom workout splits (Push/Pull/Legs, Upper/Lower, Bro Split, etc.)
  • Use preset split templates
  • Organize exercises by training day
  • Switch between different splits

Exercise Logging

  • Large database of common exercises
  • Add custom exercises
  • Log sets, reps, and weight for each exercise
  • Swipe gestures to quickly adjust values
  • Track progress over time with visual indicators (improved/same/regressed)
  • Automatic 1RM (one-rep max) estimation

Cardio Training

Activity Types

  • Walk/Run - Outdoor with GPS tracking
  • Cycling - Outdoor with GPS tracking
  • Treadmill - Indoor without GPS

Real-Time Tracking Features

  • GPS route tracking with map visualization
  • Distance measurement (miles or kilometers)
  • Elapsed time with timer
  • Calorie burn calculation based on activity type
  • Pause and resume workouts
  • View your route on a map after completion
  • Share cardio workouts to your feed

5. Body Composition Tracking

Manual Tracking

  • Log body weight
  • Record body fat percentage
  • Track muscle mass percentage
  • Support for various measurement methods (calipers, DEXA, etc.)

AI Body Analysis

  • Upload front, side, and back photos
  • AI-powered body fat percentage estimation
  • Get instant body composition feedback
  • Track changes over time

Fitness Class System

Class Description
Cutting Focused on fat loss while preserving muscle
Bulking Focused on muscle gain with caloric surplus
Maintenance Maintaining current physique
Recomp Simultaneous fat loss and muscle gain

Training Partners

7. Challenges & Competitions

Challenge Types

10. Boost System

Top-ranked users can boost posts to increase their visibility across the entire platform.

Boost Allocations (per year)

Rank Annual Boosts
#1 15 boosts
#2-3 10 boosts
#4-5 5 boosts
#6-10 3 boosts

Boosted posts appear in everyone's feed, not just training partners. Boosts reset annually on January 1st.
