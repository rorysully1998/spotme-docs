Allowed Content

What We Love to See

  • Progress photos and updates: Share your fitness journey!
  • Workout achievements: PRs, completed workouts, fitness milestones
  • Meal prep and nutrition: Healthy food photos and recipes
  • Motivational content: Inspire others on their journey
  • Challenge updates: Share your competition progress
  • Fitness tips: Help others with what you've learned
  • Questions and discussions: Ask for advice, share experiences

Photo Guidelines

  • Fitness-related photos (gym selfies, progress pics, meals, outdoor workouts)
  • Appropriate workout attire (what you'd wear in a public gym)
  • Before/after transformation photos
  • Food and meal photos
  • Outdoor activity photos

Prohibited Content

The following content is absolutely not allowed on SpotMe:

1. Nudity and Sexual Content

  • Full or partial nudity
  • Sexually explicit or suggestive content
  • Sexual solicitation

Note: Fitness attire for progress photos is acceptable (sports bras, compression shorts, swimwear, etc.)

2. Violence and Dangerous Activities

  • Graphic violence or gore
  • Threats of violence
  • Promoting dangerous exercises without proper safety warnings
  • Self-harm or suicide content
  • Encouraging dangerous weight loss practices

3. Hate and Harassment

  • Hate speech based on race, ethnicity, religion, gender, sexual orientation, disability, or other protected characteristics
  • Bullying or targeted harassment
  • Body shaming or appearance-based attacks
  • Doxing (sharing others' private information)

4. Illegal Activities

  • Drug use or promotion
  • Illegal steroid promotion or sales
  • Underage drinking
  • Any illegal activity

5. Misinformation

  • Dangerous health misinformation
  • Fake progress photos or results
  • Deceptive before/after photos
  • Promoting products with false claims

6. Spam and Deception

  • Spam or repetitive posting
  • Fake accounts or impersonation
  • Pyramid schemes or MLM promotion
  • Unsolicited advertising

7. Minor Safety

  • Any content that exploits or endangers minors
  • Inappropriate content involving anyone under 18

Challenge and Competition Guidelines

Fair Play Rules

  1. Be Honest: Report accurate workout data
  2. No Cheating: Don't manipulate or falsify results
  3. Good Sportsmanship: Win or lose with grace
  4. Respect Opponents: Competition should be fun, not hostile

What's Not Okay

  • Trash talking that crosses into harassment
  • Manipulating app data to gain unfair advantages
  • Creating fake accounts to boost challenge stats
  • Betting real money through the app

Commenting Guidelines

Good Comments

  • Encouragement and support
  • Constructive fitness advice
  • Genuine questions
  • Celebrating others' achievements

Bad Comments

  • Unsolicited criticism of appearance
  • Negative comments about someone's body
  • Harassment or bullying
  • Spam or self-promotion
  • Sexual comments

Reporting Content

How to Report

If you see content that violates these guidelines:

  1. Tap the menu icon (...) on the post
  2. Select "Report Post"
  3. Choose the reason for reporting
  4. Submit your report

What Happens After a Report

  • Reports are reviewed by our team
  • Reported content may be removed if it violates guidelines
  • Repeat offenders may have their accounts suspended or banned
  • We do not reveal who made reports

When to Report

Please report:

  • Content that violates these guidelines
  • Accounts that appear to be fake or spam
  • Harassment directed at you or others
  • Any content that makes you feel unsafe

Consequences for Violations

Warning System

  1. First Violation: Warning and content removal
  2. Second Violation: Temporary posting restriction
  3. Third Violation: Extended suspension
  4. Severe/Repeated Violations: Permanent ban

Immediate Ban Offenses

Some violations result in immediate permanent bans:

  • Child exploitation content (also reported to authorities)
  • Credible threats of violence
  • Severe harassment
  • Illegal activity

Appeals

If you believe your content was removed in error or your account was wrongly suspended:

  1. Contact us at sullivanrory9@gmail.com
  2. Include your username and explanation
  3. We will review and respond within a reasonable timeframe

Tips for Positive Participation

  1. Be supportive: A simple "great job!" can make someone's day
  2. Share knowledge: If you've learned something helpful, pass it on
  3. Stay humble: Everyone starts somewhere
  4. Be authentic: Real progress beats fake perfection
  5. Protect your privacy: Be mindful of what you share
  6. Report, don't retaliate: If someone breaks the rules, report them instead of engaging

A Note on Body Image

We understand fitness involves discussing bodies and physical changes. We encourage:

  • Focusing on health, strength, and how you feel
  • Celebrating all body types
  • Avoiding comparison culture
  • Being sensitive when commenting on others' bodies

Remember: Everyone's fitness journey is different. What works for one person may not work for another. Let's support each other in becoming our healthiest selves.
