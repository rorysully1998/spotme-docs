Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my macros?

Navigate to the Calorie Tracker tab, tap the + button, and either search our food database, scan a nutrition label with your camera, or enter values manually.

How do I log a workout?

Go to the Motion tab where you can log gym sessions with exercises, sets, reps, and weights. You can also track cardio activities like running, walking, and cycling.

How does the AI body analysis work?

Upload a photo and our AI will estimate your body composition including body fat percentage and muscle mass. This is an estimate and should not replace professional assessments.

Can I sync with Apple Health?

Yes! SpotMe integrates with Apple HealthKit to sync your workouts, calories, and activity data. Enable this in your profile settings under Health Sync.

How do I change my privacy settings?

Go to your Profile, tap the settings icon, and you can control which stats are visible to other users (age, weight, height, body fat, muscle mass, fitness class).

How do I delete my account?

Go to Profile > Settings > Delete Account. This will permanently remove all your data from our servers. You can also email us to request account deletion.

How do challenges work?

You can challenge other users to fitness competitions. Go to a user's profile and tap "Challenge" to set up a workout or nutrition challenge with custom terms.

What are Training Partners?

Training Partners is our follow system. Follow other users to see their posts in your feed and stay motivated together.
